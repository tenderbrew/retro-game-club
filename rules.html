Rules & FAQ

How It Works

Every month, the Retro Game Club picks one retro game for the community to play together. Members play at their own pace, discuss the game on Discord, and earn trophies for completing challenges.

Monthly Schedule

Week 1 & 2 Play the game of the month
Week 3 Keep playing, discuss and submit screenshots and gallery art
Week 4 Keep playing, vote on next month's game

How Games Are Chosen

  1. Members nominate games over a couple of days during the final week of the month.
  2. A first-round vote narrows down the list.
  3. If one game wins by a landslide, it's locked in.
  4. If several games are close, additional rounds are held until a winner emerges.

Trophy System
